# Video Prompt Example

## Input

- 产品名称: 黑色保温杯
- 品牌调性: 克制、通勤、家居咖啡感
- 使用场景: 早晨出门前的餐桌
- 平台: 抖音
- 输出类型: 视频Prompt

## Output

### Runway / Kling / Luma / Pika Universal Prompt

`Create a vertical 9:16 short video for a black travel tumbler in a quiet home cafe still life scene, morning table before leaving home, minimal lifestyle product photography, soft natural side window light, warm neutral cinematic atmosphere, warm wood tabletop, off-white linen, muted ceramic cup, small notebook, subtle black accents, generous negative space, shallow depth of field, low contrast shadows, realistic contact shadows. The camera starts behind a blurred coffee cup, slowly slides right to reveal the tumbler, then gently pushes in. Light softly changes across the product surface, calm rhythm, no aggressive zoom. Keep the product shape, proportions, packaging, logo, label, typography, readable text, cap, material, and color unchanged. Maintain product identity consistently across all frames; no morphing, no label drift, no text changes, no packaging deformation. No extra text, no subtitles, no UI overlay, no watermark, no frontal face.`

`hard sell advertising, e-commerce studio, Taobao product shot, high saturation background, harsh flash, neon colors, fast aggressive zoom, shaky camera, distorted product, warped label, changed logo, misspelled text, packaging deformation, product morphing, label drift, extra text, subtitles, UI overlay, watermark, price tag, frontal face, cluttered table, unrealistic shadows, floating product, plastic texture, low quality, blurry, noisy`

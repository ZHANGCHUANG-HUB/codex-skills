# Single Photo Example

## Input

- 产品名称: 手冲咖啡豆包装
- 品牌调性: 安静、手作、日常高级
- 使用场景: 周末早晨的窗边咖啡桌
- 平台: 小红书
- 输出类型: 单张产品摄影图

## Output

### 中文说明

画面是一张窗边 home cafe 静物图。咖啡豆包装放在木桌偏右位置，旁边有陶瓷杯、亚麻布和一本半开的书。整体克制、温暖、有生活痕迹，产品包装保持清晰可识别，不添加任何画面文字。

### English Image Prompt

`A coffee bean package placed naturally on a window-side wooden table during a quiet weekend morning, quiet home cafe still life photography, minimal lifestyle product photography, soft natural window light from the side, warm neutral cinematic atmosphere, off-white linen, muted gray-brown ceramic cup, half-open book, subtle black coffee accent, generous negative space, asymmetrical composition, foreground blur, shallow depth of field, realistic contact shadows, low contrast soft shadows, calm lived-in editorial mood. Keep the product shape, proportions, packaging, logo, label, typography, readable text, cap, material, and color unchanged. Do not warp, redesign, relabel, recolor, duplicate, crop, or obscure the product. No extra text in the image.`

### Negative Prompt

`hard sell advertising, Taobao studio product photography, e-commerce packshot, high saturation background, neon colors, harsh flash, glossy artificial studio light, distorted product, warped label, incorrect logo, misspelled text, changed packaging, duplicate product, cropped product, extra text, subtitles, UI overlay, watermark, price tag, frontal face, low quality, blurry, noisy`

### 推荐比例

`4:5`

### 镜头参数

50mm full-frame equivalent, f/2.8, tabletop side angle, product label in focus.

### 光线描述

Soft side window light, warm morning tone, gentle falloff, low contrast shadows.

### 构图说明

产品偏右，左侧保留大面积留白；前景用杯沿做轻微虚化遮挡，背景书页轻微失焦。

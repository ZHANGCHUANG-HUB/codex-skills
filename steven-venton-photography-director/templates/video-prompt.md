# Video Prompt Template

## Universal Video Prompt

`Create a vertical 9:16 short video for [PRODUCT_NAME] in quiet home cafe still life photography style, minimal lifestyle product photography, soft natural window light, warm neutral cinematic atmosphere. The scene takes place in [USAGE_SCENE], with warm wood, off-white linen, muted gray-brown ceramics, and subtle black accents. Use slow cinematic camera movement, gentle push-ins, lateral slides, foreground blur, shallow depth of field, low contrast shadows, and believable everyday details. Keep the product shape, proportions, packaging, logo, label, typography, readable text, cap, material, and color unchanged. Maintain product identity consistently across all frames; no morphing, no label drift, no text changes, no packaging deformation. No extra text, subtitles, UI overlay, watermark, or frontal face.`

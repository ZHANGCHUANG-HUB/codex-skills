# Nine-Shot Storyboard Template

Use exactly 9 shots for vertical 9:16 short video.

Product protection line for every English prompt:

`Keep the product shape, proportions, packaging, logo, label, typography, readable text, cap, material, and color unchanged. Do not warp, redesign, relabel, recolor, duplicate, crop, or obscure the product. No extra text in the image.`

Video protection line:

`Maintain product identity consistently across all frames; no morphing, no label drift, no text changes, no packaging deformation.`

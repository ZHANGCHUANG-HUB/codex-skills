# Single Photo Prompt Template

## 中文说明

为「[产品名称]」生成一张安静生活方式静物摄影图。画面发生在「[使用场景]」，整体是 home cafe / minimal lifestyle product photography 氛围。产品自然放置在生活场景中，保持外观比例、包装、logo、标签文字和材质不变，不做硬广式突出。

## English Image Prompt

`[PRODUCT_NAME] placed naturally in a quiet home cafe still life scene, [USAGE_SCENE], minimal lifestyle product photography, soft natural window light from the side, warm neutral cinematic atmosphere, Japanese minimal interior, Nordic home styling, warm wood tabletop, off-white linen, muted gray-brown ceramics, subtle black accents, generous negative space, asymmetrical composition, foreground blur, shallow depth of field, realistic contact shadows, low contrast soft shadows, tactile everyday details, editorial still life photography, calm and lived-in mood. Keep the product shape, proportions, packaging, logo, label, typography, readable text, cap, material, and color unchanged. Do not warp, redesign, relabel, recolor, duplicate, crop, or obscure the product. No extra text in the image.`

## Negative Prompt

`hard sell advertising, Taobao studio product photography, e-commerce packshot, high saturation background, neon colors, harsh flash, glossy artificial studio light, over-sharpened, HDR, plastic texture, distorted product, warped label, incorrect logo, misspelled text, changed packaging, duplicate product, cropped product, floating product, impossible shadow, messy clutter, dirty table, extra text, subtitles, UI overlay, watermark, price tag, discount label, poster typography, frontal face, low quality, blurry, noisy`

## 推荐比例

`4:5` for Xiaohongshu and Instagram feed, `9:16` for cover or short-video use, `3:4` for quiet editorial product image.

## 镜头参数

- 45mm or 50mm full-frame equivalent.
- f/2.8-f/4 for product clarity with soft background separation.
- Camera slightly above tabletop or at low side angle.

## 光线描述

Soft side window light, warm morning or late-afternoon tone, gentle falloff, low contrast shadows, no hard flash.

## 构图说明

Off-center product placement, large negative space, foreground obstruction such as curtain edge or cup rim, shallow depth, readable label, believable contact shadow.

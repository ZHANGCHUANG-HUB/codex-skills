# Cover Image Prompt Template

## 中文说明

为「[产品名称]」生成一张适合「[平台]」的竖版封面图。封面不是广告海报，不添加标题或文字，而是以安静生活方式静物摄影制造停留感。产品保持清晰可识别，画面有大量留白，可以后续在平台内另行加字。

## English Cover Prompt

`Vertical 9:16 cover image for [PRODUCT_NAME], quiet home cafe still life photography, minimal lifestyle product photography, soft natural window light from the side, warm neutral cinematic atmosphere, product placed off-center with generous negative space, warm wood tabletop, off-white linen, muted gray-brown ceramics, subtle black accents, foreground blur, shallow depth of field, realistic contact shadows, calm editorial composition, lived-in home cafe mood. Keep the product shape, proportions, packaging, logo, label, typography, readable text, cap, material, and color unchanged. Do not warp, redesign, relabel, recolor, duplicate, crop, or obscure the product. No extra text in the image.`

## Negative Prompt

`poster design, headline text, slogan, subtitles, UI overlay, watermark, price tag, discount label, hard sell advertising, Taobao studio product photography, high saturation background, harsh flash, neon colors, distorted product, changed logo, misspelled label, warped package, duplicate product, cropped product, clutter, frontal face, low quality, blurry, noisy`

## Composition Notes

- Keep upper or side negative space clean.
- Product can sit in lower third or off-center third.
- Avoid central poster layout unless requested.
- Make the first impression quiet, tactile, and premium.

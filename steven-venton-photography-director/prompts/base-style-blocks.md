# Base Style Blocks

## Core Style

`quiet home cafe still life photography, minimal lifestyle product photography, soft natural window light, warm neutral cinematic atmosphere`

## Expanded Style

`quiet home cafe still life photography, minimal lifestyle product photography, soft natural window light from a nearby window, warm neutral cinematic atmosphere, Japanese minimal interior, Nordic home styling, restrained everyday calmness, warm wood, off-white linen, muted gray-brown ceramics, subtle black accents, generous negative space, asymmetrical composition, shallow depth of field, foreground blur, low contrast soft shadows, realistic contact shadows, tactile everyday details, editorial still life composition, calm lived-in mood`

## Product Protection

`Keep the product shape, proportions, packaging, logo, label, typography, readable text, cap, material, and color unchanged. Do not warp, redesign, relabel, recolor, duplicate, crop, or obscure the product.`

## Video Product Protection

`Maintain product identity consistently across all frames; no morphing, no label drift, no text changes, no packaging deformation.`

## No Text Rule

`No extra text, no subtitles, no UI overlay, no watermark, no price tag, no discount label, no poster typography in the image.`

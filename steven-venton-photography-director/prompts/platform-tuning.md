- Stronger first frame, clear product reveal within the first 1-2 shots.
- Slightly more movement: slow push-in, reveal through foreground, hand action.

`clear first-frame product reveal, calm but attention-holding vertical composition, slow cinematic motion, tactile home cafe details`

`editorial Xiaohongshu-style lifestyle still life, diary-like home cafe realism, refined neutral props, calm aspirational everyday mood`

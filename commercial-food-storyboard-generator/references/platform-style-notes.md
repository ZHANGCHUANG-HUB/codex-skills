# Platform Style Notes

Use these notes only when the platform affects the creative direction.

## 抖音

- Prioritize an immediate visual hook in shot 1.
- Use faster motion language: pour, steam, sparkle, cut, lift, reveal, clink.
- Make texture and appetite cues very explicit.
- Favor bright warm highlights, energetic camera moves, and strong product payoff.

## 小红书

- Favor editorial lifestyle styling and a shareable, aspirational mood.
- Include refined tabletop details: linen, ceramic plates, glassware, flowers, menu cards, handwritten notes.
- Keep language polished and sensory rather than hard-selling.
- Use soft warm window light, shallow depth of field, and tasteful negative space.

## Instagram Reels

- Favor cinematic continuity, premium brand polish, and globally readable visuals.
- Use elegant camera moves: slow push-in, parallax slide, rack focus, macro-to-wide reveal.
- Keep props minimal and high-end.
- Make the final hero shot suitable for a clean ad end frame.
